Motor for Any Vehicle

Tailored to Your Specs!

Design motors for the vehicle by inputting key specifications like battery voltage, wheel radius, and gross weight. Define performance parameters, such as acceleration, max speed, and performance on gradients. Customize the vehicle’s drive cycle interactively by adjusting points on a Speed vs. Time graph for precise control.

Instantly Compute and
Visualize the

Power, Torque, and Performance

MotorMojo computes and displays peak torque, power, and speed, along with the rated operating points, in both numerical and graphical formats. It also analyzes drive cycle performance, providing an enhanced and interactive experience for users.

Effortless Motor Design:

Tailor Specs, Geometry, and Cost for Any Application

Design motors for any application, including EVs, starting from output specifications. Instantly generate motor geometry, winding diagrams, flux density plots, and estimates for cost and weight, all tailored to match the vehicle’s performance needs.

Visualize Motor Performance:

Torque, Efficiency, and More

MotorMojo offers detailed plots including torque vs. speed, efficiency, winding temperature, motor constraint curves, and maximum torque trajectory.
